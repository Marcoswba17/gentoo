A practical retail labor platform

Gentoo RX helps supermarkets solve labor pressure with a managed shelf-stocking system.

We provide labor services to supermarkets around the globe. By combining specialized robots, telecommunications, and trained human monitoring, Gentoo RX creates a practical system for stocking supermarket shelves while improving cost efficiency for customers.

Operating model

Robots perform the physical shelf work. People stay in control.

Lease

Robots leased to supermarkets

Gentoo RX leases specialized shelf-stocking robots to supermarkets, lowering the need for large upfront equipment investment.

Control

Remote monitoring center

Operators monitor and control the robots from a remote center, allowing labor to be organized efficiently across different parts of the globe.

Train

Employee training included

We train the employees who monitor the robots in each supermarket, helping teams operate the system with confidence.

Support

Maintenance and upgrades

Supermarket labor challenges

Supermarkets need a more efficient way to staff repetitive shelf-stocking work.

Cost

High labor costs

Store operators face rising labor expense, especially for repetitive stocking work that must be completed reliably.

Turnover

Unstable labor force

Supermarkets often struggle with high employee turnover, making staffing harder to plan and control.

Health

Night-shift strain

Employees working overnight shifts can encounter physical and emotional health issues, including fatigue and depression.

Labor pool

Shortest labor pool

Many markets have a limited pool of people willing and available to perform overnight shelf-stocking work.

Efficiency

Cost-efficient service

Remote-controlled robotics helps optimize labor costs while maintaining a satisfactory service for supermarkets.

Customer

Customer satisfaction

Our responsibility is to provide an efficient system that helps supermarkets satisfy their customers.

Remote-control workflow

A managed service that connects robots, telecom, and trained people.

Specialized robots perform the physical work required to stock shelves while trained employees monitor the tasks through remote control. By placing remote-control capability in different parts of the globe, Gentoo RX can optimize labor costs for customers.

  • Robots handle repeatable shelf-stocking tasks inside the supermarket.
  • Employees are trained to monitor and control robot activity.
  • Maintenance, replacement, and newer model upgrades are managed by Gentoo RX.
