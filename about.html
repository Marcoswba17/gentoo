About Gentoo RX

We believe better retail
starts with better execution.

Gentoo RX Inc is a robotics company developing a managed, human-guided approach to shelf operations for supermarkets and grocery retailers.

Our thesis

The physical shelf still matters.

Commerce may be increasingly digital, but grocery retail remains deeply physical. Availability, freshness, presentation, and labor all converge in the aisle. When execution breaks down, revenue and trust can disappear in the same moment.

Our mission is to give retailers a reliable new layer of operating capacity—using robotics for physical work and people for judgment.

Human-centeredTechnology should extend people and improve the work around them.
Outcome-orientedMeasure success in availability, consistency, and operational value.
Globally scalableBuild systems and teams that can serve stores across markets.

Why Gentoo
